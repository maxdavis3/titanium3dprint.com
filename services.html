Our Services

Comprehensive 3d metal printing solutions tailored to your needs

WHAT WE OFFER

Our Expertise

DMLS / SLM Printing

Direct Metal Laser Sintering and Selective Laser Melting for precision metal parts. Achieve 99.5% density with complex internal geometries in titanium, Inconel, and stainless steel alloys.

Rapid Prototyping

Fast-turn metal prototypes from CAD to finished part. Validate designs before committing to tooling — parts delivered in days, not weeks, with full mechanical property data.

Post-Processing

Full post-processing suite: support removal, heat treatment, HIP at 2000°F / 15,000 psi, CNC finishing, and surface treatment to meet your application requirements.

Quality Inspection

CMM dimensional verification, CT scanning, and material certification. Every part ships with a full quality report and mechanical property documentation.
