Frequently Asked Questions

What information do I need to provide for a quote?

Ideally, CAD files (STEP, IGES, or STL). At minimum: part dimensions, material requirements, tolerances, and application. We can work with sketches for initial consultation.

How long does the manufacturing process take?

Standard turnaround: 3-4 weeks. Expedited (1-2 weeks available with 25% surcharge. Timeline includes build, stress relief, HIP (if needed), finishing, and testing.

Do you offer HIP treatment?

Yes. HIP (Hot Isostatic Pressing) is included in our medical device services. Optional for aerospace applications. Eliminates residual porosity, achieving 100% density.

What certifications do you maintain?

ISO 9001:2015, ISO 5832-3 (medical titanium), ASTM F136 (medical devices), ITAR (defense), AMS 4928 (aerospace).

Can you manufacture patient-specific medical implants?

Yes. We work with medical device companies to convert CT scans into CAD, then produce custom-fitted titanium implants with full biocompatibility verification.

What's the minimum order quantity?

One part. We consolidate builds to optimize costs. Batch consolidation passes savings to customers when possible.

Do you offer design optimization services?

Yes. Our DFM (Design for Manufacturability) analysis is free with quote requests. Topology optimization and lattice structure design available as paid consulting services.

How do I send CAD files securely?

Email to quote@ti3daerospace.com (encrypted), or use our secure file upload portal on request. We maintain strict IP confidentiality agreements.
